DAM runs your AI coding agent in its own Kubernetes pod — credentials safe, network sandboxed, awake when you're not. Sign in, and the get-started bar shows what's next. Click "Set up a provider" — you land on Anthropic. You've got two ways to add your credential. OAuth Token plugs into the session you get from running `claude setup-token` on your own machine. Or paste an API key directly, if you already have one provisioned. Pick the tab that matches what you have, drop it in — and you're ready for connections and agents.
